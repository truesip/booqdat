About BOOQDAT

Ticketing Built for Real-World Event Teams

We help promoters launch events quickly, sell confidently, and deliver better attendee experiences.

Our mission

BOOQDAT USA LLC was created to simplify event ticketing for independent promoters, venues, and growing production teams. Our platform is designed to reduce setup friction, improve conversion, and provide organizers with the operational clarity they need to scale events responsibly.

What we focus on

  • Fast event publishing with share-ready links
  • Secure purchase flow for attendees on any device
  • Clear ticket and attendee records for event-day operations
  • Transparent policies and responsive support
