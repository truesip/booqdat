Support

BOOQDAT support is available for both event promoters and ticket buyers.

For promoters

  • Event setup and publishing assistance
  • Ticket link troubleshooting
  • Order issue resolution and reporting support
  • General account and payout questions

For attendees

  • Order confirmation and ticket delivery checks
  • Duplicate purchase review
  • Event information clarification requests
  • General ticketing support

How to contact support

Email helloworld@booqdat.com and include your event name, order reference (if available), and a short description of the issue.
