Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 21, 2026

BOOQDAT USA LLC (“BOOQDAT,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard personal information when you use our ticketing platform.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information provided by promoters (name, contact details, business details)
  • Checkout information provided by attendees (name, email, phone, order details)
  • Usage data such as browser type, device information, and interaction logs

2. How we use information

  • To operate ticket sales, confirmations, and event records
  • To provide support and resolve transaction issues
  • To detect fraud, abuse, and unauthorized activity
  • To improve performance, reliability, and user experience

3. Sharing of information

We may share relevant order details with event promoters and payment processors as required to complete transactions and support event operations. We do not sell personal information for unrelated marketing use.

4. Data retention

We retain records for operational, legal, and accounting purposes for periods required by law or business necessity.

5. Security

We use commercially reasonable safeguards to protect data against unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. No method of transmission or storage is fully guaranteed.

6. Your choices

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of eligible personal data, subject to legal limitations.

7. Children’s privacy

Our platform is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children without legal authorization.
